Careers in Rhetorics of Science, Technology + Culture and Multimedia Communications

The RSTC major and Multimedia Communications minor work very well together in the career field, as require very similar skills within them. This also means that the job market expands greatly when you have background in both RSTC and communication. Here is a list of possible careers you could explore with this major and minor combination.

  • Publisher
  • Editorial Assitant
  • Managing Editor
  • Copywriter
  • Grant and Proposal Writer
  • Technical Writer
  • Research Assistant
  • Human-computer Interface Designer
  • Writer of Hardware and Software Documentation
  • Visual Designer
  • Web Page Developer
  • Multimedia Journalist
  • Video and Multimedia Producer
  • Social Media Specialist
  • Digital Marketer
